# Data Input Mode

## Transfer to NASC

At this stage, the UAS data transfer to NASC is carried out **twice a month**, approximately on the **4th and 16th** of each month as part of the reports of the current Antarctic expedition and contains only primary data.

Technically, the reports are transferred by uploading data from the UAS to the **Google Drive** cloud storage.

- **Upload on the 16th** contains only variations
- **Upload on the 4th** contains variations, absolute values, and a text report

## Transfer to Intermagnet

The transmission of UAS data to the international **Intermagnet** network is carried out in accordance with the procedures described at <https://tech-man.intermagnet.org/stable/chapters/submitdata/toc_submitdata.html>.

**Transmission characteristics:**

- Data in **IAGA-2002** format are transmitted **daily** with a delay of **2 days**
- Baselines are determined based on the results of the year and are transmitted **by July 1** of the following year

## Transfer to ISGI

The UAS data transmission to the International Geomagnetic Index Center **ISGI** is carried out **monthly**.

Data is transmitted **by e-mail**.
